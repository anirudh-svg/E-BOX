ACADEMIC USE CASES

AI suitable for academia.

EBox runs text, speech, vision, and multimodal models, on-premise, on cloud, or on edge, all
configurable to your institution's workflows.

Hands-on AI development for students

Move beyond calling APIs. Students run real models, tune inference, manage deployments, and build applications on AI infrastructure all on campus.

e.g. A final-year project team deploys a fine-tuned NLP model on the campus server, no cloud account, no per-token cost.

Research with full data sovereignty

Sensitive research datasets (medical, behavioural, linguistic) never leave the institution. Run AI on your research data without exposing it to external services or third-party terms of service.

e.g. A public health research team processes patient survey data entirely on-premise, in full compliance with institutional review requirements.

Multilingual AI for diverse student communities

Deploy models that work in the languages students actually speak. EBox supports multilingual inference, making AI tools accessible regardless of language of instruction or regional background.

e.g. A university deploys a model supporting student queries in Tamil, Hindi, and English simultaneously.

AI-assisted research and literature review

Summarise papers, extract findings, analyse datasets, and generate structured research outputs, entirely within the university's secure environment.

e.g. PhD researchers summarise 200 papers for a systematic literature review using EBox, with all document data remaining on the university premises.

Campus-wide AI services

Serve multiple departments, faculties, and student bodies from a single EBox deployment using built-in multi-tenancy, each with isolated access and its own usage visibility.

e.g. Engineering, Law, and Medical faculties each access dedicated model instances from one deployment.
